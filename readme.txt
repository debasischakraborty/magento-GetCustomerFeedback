Get Customer Feedback

BETA TEST Module

This extensions sends a Get Customer Feedback email to customers who have placed an order.


To install the extension, copy the contents of the extension app folder to your magento store app folder.
Make sure the extension cache folder and subfolders are writeable.
Refresh your magento cache.
Logout from admin, and login again.
